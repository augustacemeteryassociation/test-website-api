Vision Statement

The Augusta Cemetery Association is dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and conservation of the East and West Lawn Cemeteries.

Mission Statement

It is the mission of the Augusta Cemetery Association to preserve, restore and maintain the East and West Lawn Cemeteries out of respect for those who have gone before us and our community at large.
